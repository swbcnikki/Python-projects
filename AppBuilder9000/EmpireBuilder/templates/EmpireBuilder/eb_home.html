The Train Ride...

Thank you for choosing to travel with us on this 3 day - 2 night timeless adventure. What a great opportunity to make memories on a solo trip or with friends and/or family. You will see some of the most beautiful sites in America through your big picture window as we roll by. We will stop at different stations along the way. Some will be a short pick-up and/or drop-off of passengers, while others will allow between 10 and 25 minutes of time to walk around outside. The details will be provided by announcement and attendants can answer any questions you have. Please reach out to our customer service department with any questions by email, chat or phone.
