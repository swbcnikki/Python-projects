The Different Kinds of Music

Music can come in many styles and genres. It can range from simple classic to extreme death metal.
As various as music can be, so can its followers. Music taste is part of our own unique identity. The best part?
We're not limited to picking just one genre! People can like as many music genres as they please, or even just
a song or two from a genre without liking all or most songs in that particular genre. With music, there are no
limits!
