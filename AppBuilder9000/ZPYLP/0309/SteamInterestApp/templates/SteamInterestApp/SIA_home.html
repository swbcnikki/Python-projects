Ever look at your Steam library and think, "Man, I have a lot of games but don't know what to play.."

Everyone knows that Steam games are like Pokemon: You gotta catch them all! Yet, with all the great sales Steam provides, it's hard to keep track of your library and what you want to play next...

That's where the Steam Interest App comes in!

This app provides an easy way to select what Steam game you want to play next! Just enter all the games you have in your Steam library into this app's database which accounts for:

  1. Title of game
  2. Genre
  3. Date Released
  4. Date Purchased
  5. Interest Level

With this list, you can easily keep track of your games while deciding on what game to play next!

Theoretically, in the future, this app will connect to your Steam account, bypassing the need to enter data yourself. All you would need to do is put your interest level in the game, which would be initially determined by purchase date vs. current date.

Looking for some games to add to your Steam library? Why not check out GameSpot's 2020 recommendations?
