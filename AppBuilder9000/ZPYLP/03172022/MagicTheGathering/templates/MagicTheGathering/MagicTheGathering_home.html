About Magic the Gathering!

Welcome to the wonderful world of Magic The Gathering! Magic is a head-to-head collectable card game! It's filled with fantasy and strategy to can appeal to any individual! One of the largest parts about MTG is keeping your cards organized and sorted! This app helps keep track of the cards you own by allowing you to put them in a database and then search them up at a whim! Feel free to look into the About page if you're looking to learn more about the game. Otherwise, have fun starting your collection!!
