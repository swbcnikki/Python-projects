Basketball Stats!

This app utilizes the Django Framework. The "Add Players" page allows users to create a "Player" and a few stats about that "Player." The "Players Added" page displays all "Players" created using the form from the "Add Players" page. The API section of this app has a "Standings" and a "Conferences & Divisions" page. "Standings" shows the NBA standings for specific years, including the current season. "Conferences & Divisions" displays all of the teams in their respective divisions, and it also shows each team's logo separated into their divisions. The Web Scraping section includes "NBA History" and the Portland Trail Blazers' Roster. "NBA History" displays a list of previous NBA Champions, who they defeated, and who won the Finals MVP. The "Portland Trail Blazers Roster" shows the current roster according to Basketball-reference. The section under Favorites has an "Add Favorites" page that allows users to save their favorite NBA team and a "Favorite Teams" page to display all of the user's favorite teams. Clicking on a favorite team will open up the details page of the team. Info about the team is scraped from Basketball-reference and is displayed on the team details page.
