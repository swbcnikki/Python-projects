Big 5 Personality Manager

This app is designed to help you understand your personality and to compare it to others. Click "Discover your Personality" to take a test based on the Big 5 Personality theory with questions taken from the International Personality Item Pool. You will receive a report on your results and may search for jobs by location that correspond with your interests.


Developed by Carson Cook.


This project was built with the Django Framework and Python to demonstrate proficiency with basic CRUD operations, API functionality and web scraping with Beautiful Soup.
