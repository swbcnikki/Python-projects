Star Watch

A personal astronomy assistant






For countless generations human-kind has gazed into the night sky with an unyielding sense of wonder. They are the furnaces of all creation that illuminate a vastly dark universe and have been given constant praise for what they provide. Staring into the depth of the cosmos is a truly daunting expedition, but Star Watch is supposed to help with that. While I still have to plan out future features, Star Watch will be an app designed to asist the user in tracking and identifying stars based on their brightness and position in the sky.
