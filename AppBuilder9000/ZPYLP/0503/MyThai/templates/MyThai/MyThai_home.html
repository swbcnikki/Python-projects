Welcome to MyThai!

This app was created to store your favorite (as well as your least favorite)

Thai food restaurants. The app will store the restaurants you enter, along with

a rating for that restaurant.


The app will also store any dishes you enter, and again, a rating to remind you which

dish from a restaurant is your favorite. This also allows you to sort through a

type of dish or even a specific dish. Quickly find which place had your favorite

Pad Thai or Yellow Curry.
