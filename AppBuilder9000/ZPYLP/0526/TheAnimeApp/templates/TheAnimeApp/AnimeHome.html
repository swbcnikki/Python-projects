As many may know Anime comes from Japan, it consists of many different genres. Some of which are Shounen(targets an audience that wants to chase their goals/dreams), slice of life(day to day activities for characters, laid back) and psychological thriller(involves suspense and mystery) these are just a few of many!

The art style can vary from anime to anime. Some people may have a preference with what art style of anime they like to watch, some art style can be traditional like Naruto and others can be a bit more outlandish like Jojo's Bizzare Adventure. When it comes to watching anime some might enjoy any art style, which is common!

Now something not a lot of people talk about is the anime's soundtrack, for example: Demon Slayer and Naruto in my subjective opinion have phenomenal soundtracks. This on top of the other things mentioned plus more can be a factor when it comes to rating an anime.
