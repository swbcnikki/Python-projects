Welcome to Artist Track where you can enter your favorite songs into our database, categorize them into playlists,
and find lyrics for your favorite songs and artist information for your favorite artists.

  • Click Add Song on the Nav Bar to add your songs to our database
  • Click Add Playlist to add your custom playlists.
  • Use the ctrl key to select as many songs as you like for your playlist,
  • A playlist can have as many songs as you like, and your songs can be on multiple playlists.
  • From the library tab, click on a song or playlist for more options, and to edit details.
  • Also from the library tab, click on an artist to see a details page from that artist, provided by Wikipedia.
  • Enjoy!
